⬡ V-Coordinator Help

Use this builder to import people and room data, preview each sheet, and export a print-ready workbook.

How To Import Data

People and room data can be mixed in the same CSV upload or paste block.

Smith J, 11:00, 13:30
Doe A, 11:00, 11:30, 14:00, 15:30

11:00, , 3, 2
11:30, Taylor C, 4, 1
12:00, Williams D, 2, 3
12:30, Brown E, 5, 2
13:00, Davis F, 3, 1

Person: Name, start, end or Name, start1, end1, start2, end2

Room: time, officiator, brothers, sisters

  • Room 1 (11:00 AM) officiator is always AM Shift — leave blank
  • Lines starting with # are ignored
  • Valid times: 11:00 through 16:45 in 15-minute increments

Build And Preview

AM and PM show different rooms. Switch tabs to preview each sheet, then click Build Schedule to render the current sheet.

Turn on Auto if you want the preview to rebuild automatically whenever names or room data change.

Export

Export downloads one workbook containing both AM and PM sheets, ready for printing on separate pages.

Availability Rules

The builder accepts quarter-hour entry times, but the schedule still displays 30-minute room slots.

A person must cover the full 30-minute room slot to count as available for that room.
